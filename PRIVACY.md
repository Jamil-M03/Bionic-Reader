# Privacy Policy - Bionic Reader

_Last updated: June 2026_

**Bionic Reader does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What the extension does

Bionic Reader applies "bionic reading" formatting (bolding the first part of
each word) to text on web pages and in its built-in PDF reader, to help guide
your eyes while reading.

## Data collection

None. The extension does not collect, log, or transmit any information about
you, your browsing, the pages you visit, or the documents you open.

- **No analytics or telemetry.**
- **No remote servers.** The extension makes no network requests and has no backend.
- **No third parties.** No data is shared with anyone.

## Local processing

All formatting happens locally, inside your browser:

- Web page text is restyled in place by a content script.
- PDFs you open in the reader are processed entirely on your device using the
  bundled pdf.js library. The file never leaves your computer and is never uploaded.

## Permissions

- **Storage**: used only to remember your settings (whether bionic mode is on,
  and your chosen fixation strength) on your own device. These settings are
  stored locally and are never transmitted.
- **Host permission (access to websites)**: required so the extension can apply
  formatting to whatever page you are reading. It only restyles visible text; it
  does not read, collect, or send page contents anywhere.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository.
